# AI-first client copy principle

**Why:** Joe confirmed that SAGEMRO's early user adoption depends on making AI the first thing visitors experience and understand. The platform is not just a ticket system or document repository — it is an AI-assisted equipment service platform.

## Guidelines

- First screen must clearly communicate that SAGEMRO AI helps organize equipment issues before service decisions
- Copy emphasizes: AI symptom intake, missing-detail questions, risk signals, solution options, and next-step guidance
- Do NOT overclaim: final diagnosis, pricing, purchasing, and site safety decisions still require qualified review
- Apply consistently in both English and Chinese UI
- Tone: practical industrial B2B, not consumer SaaS hype

## Key copy (as of 2026-07-17)

**English headline** (WelcomePage):
> `3 minutes of AI chat. Get your solution and next steps.`

**Chinese headline** (WelcomePage):
> `跟 AI 聊三分钟，获取设备问题解决方案和下一步。`

**Page title** (App.jsx):
> `SAGEMRO — AI-Powered Equipment Service Platform`

**AI Notice** (ChatArea):
> `AI prepares the initial brief. Final diagnosis, pricing, and safety decisions follow the SAGEMRO service process.`
